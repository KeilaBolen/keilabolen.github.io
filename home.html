About Me

My professional experience and qualifications are listed in my resume, however I am and can do so much more than what a few pages can tell you. I am organized, detail oriented, focused, and driven to succeed. When given the opportunity to learn new things, I do so quickly and with great enthusiasm. I do not shy away from a challenging work environment, as that is where I thrive. I have the ability to think outside the box and can bring a fresh perspective when others face obstacles.​

I am also a Writer, Photographer, Entrepreneur, and Co-Founder of humorouslysad.com.​

I currently have several blogs I have begun working on, and have started honing my skills in web design as well as learning to write code. All are still works in progress, but I am determined, and find the challenge both motivating and stimulating.​
